## Currently building

<CardGrid>
	<Card title="Reusable learning components" icon="puzzle">
		Free interactive web components that instructors can utilise, enabling learners to play with new ideas in theory training and at home.
        **[Licensed to be free forever](/licensing/)**.
        [Explore the learning components →](/learning-components/)
	</Card>
	<Card title="Open briefing training materials" icon="open-book">
		Starting with Australian RPL briefing presentations that are also **licensed to be free forever**, improvable by you, usable (and adaptable) by anyone.
		Improvements flow back to the community improving learning for all.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Open software libraries" icon="seti:json">
        Similarly licensed software libraries for **building aviation tooling**
        for logbooks or simplifying the administration of competency-based
        training and assessment. Think of these as **free building blocks** that
        make it **cheaper** to write custom software solutions.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Affordable VR simulator setups" icon="rocket">
		Helping Australian flight training organisations adopt consumer-grade VR flight simulator
		rigs as affordable practice tools - **affordable enough for students to begin building their own**
        and learning how they can enjoy life-long scenario-based training at home.
	</Card>
</CardGrid>

<InfoCard title="Who this is for">
Everyone from Australian Part 141/142 flight training organisations through to  **pilot
instructors** or **pilots training to become instructors**, as well as
**self-directed learners** who can use open, transparent, improvable
learning and training resources.
</InfoCard>

<InfoCard title="Why">
We all want to **maximise the efficiency of our learning** (and
the time we spend preparing for learning) so that we can be **safer pilots spending
more time doing what we love**.
</InfoCard>
